Game of Thrones

This is a modified jumbotron that occupies the entire horizontal space of its parent.

Number to Guess:

There are five house Sigils associated with radom values. Click the Sigils to add a specific amount of points to your total score

To win the game you must match the total score to the random number generated at the beginning of the game.
